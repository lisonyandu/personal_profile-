My Projects

In this section I discuss some of the the projects I worked on during my undergrad

Restore Health Cancer App

Using React-native, I and my team developed an m-health solution, called cancer App. The purpose of the app is to educate, support and empower cancer patients and their family members. The app will be made for free download on both the Apple store and the Play store so it can reach and help many cancer patients.

Travel By Investec

Using .net core, I and my team developed a prototype for web application to explore a new opportunity for Investec. The platform allows Investec clients to make a range of travel packages and transact in real time using their Investec reward points. I developed the front end of the system. I made sure the UI was very beautiful, useable and very intuitive to enhance client experience.

Uni-Exchange

An online platform that offers university students and staff members an opportunity to exchange secondhand goods among each other. Rather than people disposing their items that are no longer of use to them, they can make money out of these already used items by reaching to the needy buyers through this platform. This platform also offers services like advertising events and accommodation within an institution.
